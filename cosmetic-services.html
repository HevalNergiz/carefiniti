COSMETICS SERVICES

What To Expect When You Come In For A Medical Aesthetics Appointment At Our Clinic:

At Carefiniti Health, we are proud to provide you with a non-surgical approach to restore facial balance, generate a refreshed look and optimize beauty.
Our goal is to treat the face as a whole rather than targeting specific lines or wrinkles.
We use combination of treatments including neuromodulators, dermal fillers, PRP or thread lifting to help create “your best version” of beauty by softening deep folds, smoothing out wrinkles and fine lines, restoring lost volume, enhancing facial contours without sacrificing normal expressions and with aim to maintain a natural look and feel.
Our doctors and skin consultants will review your profile and recommend the best possible solutions uniquely designed to address your needs including non-surgical treatments and medical grade skin care products to achieve and maintain your newly refreshed look.

Cosmetic Services

NEUROMODULATORS (BOTOX®, DYSPORT®)

Botox is one of many treatment options offered to address expression lines like crow’s feet, frown lines, and forehead wrinkles. Through a carefully-administered series of virtually painless injections, certain wrinkles can be effectively smoothed, to bring about a more youthful appearance to the face.
Botox treatments are fast and comfortable, and there are no visible signs of the procedure afterwards. Many patients even choose to perform this treatment during their lunch break.
If you’re considering Botox as part of your skin care regimen, don’t hesitate to contact us with any questions.

DERMAL FILLERS

At Carefiniti Health we help many patients address the signs of aging by using specialized dermal fillers. Through a series of carefully-administered treatments, our expert providers can help patients smooth out wrinkles and add more contour and definition to the face.
Having helped thousands of patients look and feel their best, the expert providers at Carefiniti Health will use their extensive expertise to help you understand how fillers will affect your appearance, and help you select the best treatment strategy in your situation.
Do not hesitate to give us a call to set up a consultation.

NON-SURGICAL DOUBLE CHIN TREATMENT (BELKYRA®)

Carefiniti Health is pleased to offer Belkyra treatments to our patients. This is a revolutionary, FDA-approved double chin treatment, which requires absolutely no surgery. Through a series of targeted injections, the fat in the double chin is gradually destroyed, creating a tighter appearance in the neck, and achieving a more aesthetically-pleasing transition between the jaw and the neck.
In many ways, using Belkyra is like sculpting a marble statue; great care must be taken to remove the right amount of fat under the chin to create an appearance that looks natural and provides the right contours.

VAMPIRE FACIAL (MICRONEEDLING)

Microneedling, also called collagen induction therapy, is a treatment effective in treating all manner of acne scarring and pitting, injury and surgical scars, skin discoloration, mild rosacea, fine lines, wrinkles, and stretch marks. Skin needling is a safe and controlled method of treatment and it can benefit all skin types.
Some celebrities like Kim Kardashian, combine PRP with other treatments like microneedling for “Vampire facials” to bring about a glow to the skin, prior to big events.
If you would like to know more about this service, do contact us.
