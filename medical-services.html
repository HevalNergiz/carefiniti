INTRAVEOUS IRON INFUSIONS

Carefiniti Health is proud to provide patients with Monoferric infusions for treatment of Iron Deficiency Anemias in adult patients who have intolerance or unresponsiveness to oral iron therapy. The advantage of Monoferric Iron is the short duration it takes to receive the infusion, 30 minutes versus 8 hours with traditional iron sucrose formulations. Most extended benefit insurances cover for Monoferric infusion.
Please contact us if you have further questions on this service.
